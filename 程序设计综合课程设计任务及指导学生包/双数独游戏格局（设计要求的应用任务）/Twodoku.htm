Twodoku

GrandGames.net → Multi-sudoku → Twodoku
Twodoku, or double Sudoku, is one of the simplest versions of Multisudoku. In Twodoku, two Sudoku puzzles are joined bya single 3x3 cell square. Given that he puzzles share only one 3x3 set of cells, some of the simplest Twodoku may sometimes allow solving each Sudoku separately.
